The Project

My daughter needed a way to share content on the internet without resorting to social media.

The Solution

A website with user authentication and image uploading and editing capabilities.

The Plan

The minimal functional requirements allowed me to focus heavily on design and experiment with some concepts that had been in my inspiration folder for weeks. I combined bright color schemes and used svg blobs to give an especially unique style to the dessins (drawings) page.
